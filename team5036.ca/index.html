We are FRC Team 5036 The Robo Devils from Sir Wilfrid Laurier Collegiate Institute in Scarborough, Ontario. Community engagement is at the heart of what we do. We are proud to be active members of the Scarborough Village community, dedicating our time to mentoring younger students, hosting workshops, and participating in local events. Our goal is to inspire and empower others while building strong connections and making a positive impact in our community.
